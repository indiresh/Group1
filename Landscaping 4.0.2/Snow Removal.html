Snow And Ice Removal Services

During Michigan’s winter, you need a reliable and responsive snow removal service to keep your property safe for employees, while allowing your business to operate with as little interruption as possible.  For this purpose, we make every effort each season to provide a high level of service, meeting both the snow-plowing and salting needs of all of our commercial customers and residential customers. Our services and policies are listed below.  Detailed cost schedules are provided on the contract for your particular property.  If you have any questions, please contact us using out About Us page.

 

 

Standard Service Contracts include:

Plowing of parking lots,including truck wells, and loading docks.
Salting of parking lots, using bulk rock salt via-medium duty dump trucks. See our Deicing page.

Optional Services Available:

Shoveling of walkways and public sidewalks, if applicable.
Ice melter application on sidewalks and entrance-ways. See our deicing page
Buckets are available to place near front, side, or rear doors if desired.  
Loader Work, including removal and re-stacking of snow piles

 

 

 

 

Two Things to Know About our Services:

Our services are automatic. In order to most efficiently and effectively service all of our customers, we initiate services based on the type, timing, and intensity of each winter weather event.  Ideally, you won’t ever need to call us.  Of course, we are always ready to respond to your requests, and are available during the winter months to serve you 24 hours a day and 7 days a week, if necessary.

Our services are customized to your needs. We know that businesses and organizations have different needs, which is why we tailor our services to your particular situation.  We develop our routes and respond to weather events based on your business’ operating hours and desired level of service.  We are flexible enough to handle almost any commercial property, from small industrial tooling shops to large retail complexes. 

If you are interested:

All-inclusive seasonal quotes are available, upon request.  Seasonal rates for both plowing and salting can be an effective way to control costs and budget for snow removal during the upcoming winter season.
