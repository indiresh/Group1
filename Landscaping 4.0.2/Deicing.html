Deicing Services

Heavy ice, freezing rain, and extreme cold can have a tremondous impact on individual communities or an entire region.  Storms can last for days, resulting in power outages, blocked or closed roads, and businesses. Make sure you are prepared by contacting us about our De-Icing services. In addition to snow plowing services, J.N.S. Landscaping also provides salting and De-icing services for commercial and residential clients. Salting and de-icing services can be part of seasonal snow management contracts or scheduled on an as-needed basis.

Road salt or Rock salt is the most common deicer used to promote safe motor vehicle travel during winter months. The most commonly used road salt sodium chloride, which is readily available, inexpensive and effectively melts snow and ice.Please contact us before winter arrives for any other questions. 

Sidewalk Saliting is available and can be added to any contract as an added cost. Sidewalks become extremely dangerous during Michigans winter months and should be treated before its too late. We us enviornmentaly firendly products to ensure safety even after winter has passed. Contact us for more infomation about deicing sidewalks at your home or buisness.
