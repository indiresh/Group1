Welcome to J.N.S. Landscaping

We would like to offer you the opportunity to work with a company where the client relationship is first priority. We are proud to have built a successful business in an industry where 32% of contractors fail in the first 5 years.

See how our services can change your lawn! (Roll over image to see our results)

 

We employ green industry professionals all dedicated to creating a better place to work and live, not only by beautifying outdoor spaces, but also through making environmentally friendly decisions. J.N.S. has been known for quality landscapes and hardscapes that have won many awards, but we also provide maintenance, lawn care and nursery services too. Through our environmental stainability initiatives we have created infiltrating water gardens that take roof runoff water and allow it to slowly infiltrate back into the ground. We also design and install green roofs, bio-swales and erosion control systems. We are dedicated to bringing the up most quality to the landscape contractor profession though sustainable practices and smart design, landscaping and maintenance
